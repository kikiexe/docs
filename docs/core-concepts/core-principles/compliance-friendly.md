# Compliance Friendly

Veilfi bridges the gap between privacy and regulatory compliance through **Zero-Knowledge KYC**, enabling institutional adoption without sacrificing user privacy.

## Why Compliance Matters

### Institutional Barriers

DAOs, treasuries, and funds need compliance:

- Regulatory requirements (SEC, FinCEN, MiCA)
- Fiduciary duties to stakeholders
- Auditor requirements
- Insurance prerequisites

**Without Compliance**: Institutions locked out of DeFi yields.
